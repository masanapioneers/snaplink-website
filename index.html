The Model

One station, two revenue engines.

Every SnapLink station does double duty. It keeps guests powered, and it puts a display in front of a captive, dwell-time audience. That's two ways a venue benefits from a single install, at whatever scale the space calls for.

Charging

Portable, not tethered.

Guests rent a battery from the station and carry it with them, no standing at a wall outlet, no missing the game to wait by a cord.

  • Guest scans a QR code or taps to pay with Stripe, no app required
  • Battery works anywhere in the venue, indoors or out
  • Returned to any station in the network when done
Advertising

A screen your guests actually stop at.

Each station's display runs rotating digital ads in a high-dwell-time spot, visible to every guest nearby, not just the ones charging.

  • Local businesses can advertise directly on your station
  • Hyper-local targeting with national network reach
  • Performance tracked, no guessing at impressions

FAQ

Common questions.

Does it really cost nothing to host a station?

Yes. There's no upfront cost, lease payment, or maintenance fee for the venue. SnapLink covers install and upkeep.

How does installation work?

Stations are plug-and-play. No construction, no electrical work, no IT setup, just power and placement.

Where does SnapLink operate?

SnapLink currently serves the DFW area. We're actively expanding and open to conversations about venues and events outside this region.

How do guests pay?

Guests scan a QR code or tap to pay with Stripe, no app download required.

How does the venue earn revenue?

Venues earn a share of rental revenue, plus a share of advertising revenue from the station's display.

Does the battery have to be returned to the same station?

No. Guests can return a rented battery to any station in the ChargerGoGo network, not just the one they rented from.

What if something goes wrong with the station?

Support is available 24/7. Any issue with the unit is handled directly, so venue staff never have to manage or troubleshoot the hardware.

Is the placement exclusive to my venue?

We offer long-term placement agreements so your venue remains the only charging option on-site, not one of several.
